About Us


As our slogan – “Your Image, Our Duty” – aptly suggests, once we get mandate to build your PR, we leave no stone unturned in establishing the best for your company’s image in the market, respective business domains.

Founded by enterpreneur Harday Gupta, The Right PR is a one-stop solution for all the requirements of your company in terms of Public Relations. In PR industry, we have a proven track record of establishing a solid PR image in the public eye.

We put a special and undistracted focus on communication both with the clients as well as the media which helps us carving the best-in-the-industry PR campaigns and strategies. We have a dedicated PR team proactively managing the services for the client and meanwhile governing healthy media alliances as well. We take pride in giving our 100% every day for our clients. We firmly believe in delivering everything we commit and likewise creating grounds for mutual trust and loyalty.

We strive to provide the best results available in the industry. As Marc Anthony once said, ‘If you do what you love, you’ll never work a day in your life’, likewise we value your concerns and are ready to serve you in the best of our capacities.

We have our corporate offices cross Jaipur, Noida and Mumbai.

Allocate your PR mandate to us, and forget the rest. As we say, Your Image, Our Duty!
